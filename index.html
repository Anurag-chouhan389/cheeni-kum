Our Story

Sweetness, measured in craftsmanship

Cheeni Kum began with a simple belief — that indulgence shouldn't come at the cost of quality. Our name is a quiet promise: a little less sugar, a lot more love, in every box that leaves our kitchen. What started as a single mithai counter has grown into a complete house of sweets, cakes, bakery and namkeen, without ever letting go of the recipes we started with.

Every laddu is hand-rolled, every cake is layered to order, and every batch of namkeen is fried in small quantities so it reaches you at its crispest. Nothing sits in our kitchen overnight — what's made today is sold today.
